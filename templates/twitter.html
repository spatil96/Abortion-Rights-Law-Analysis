Twitter Trend Analysis

The graph below aims to display data obtained from the Twitter API. The main aim of this graph is to us an insight of the data depending on the users' location so that we can analyze whether the data is location dependent or independent, especially when it comes to a crucial topic like abortion. Here the research question of analyzing tweets based on various geographic locations in the United States of America is answered. The count of tweets regarding abortion is displayed. The data was gathered over a two-month period. The graph can be changed geographically, i.e. by selecting multiple states. In this interactive tool, the user may choose from a variety of states to see and compare data coming from multiple tweets all around the world using a volume stream.
